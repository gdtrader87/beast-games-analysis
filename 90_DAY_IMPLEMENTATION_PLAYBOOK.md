# 90-Day Implementation Playbook
## Head of Analytics — Beast Industries

---

## Overview

**Objective:** Establish the analytics function and deliver measurable impact within the first 90 days.

**Phased Structure:**
- Days 1–30: Foundation — model deployment, team structure, quick wins
- Days 31–60: Optimization — A/B test results, cost reduction, international analysis
- Days 61–90: Scale — international launch, format expansion, year 2 planning

**90-Day Targets:** +18% views (8.05B → 9.5B monthly), $2.3M in identified cost savings, $600M+ growth opportunity scoped and presented.

## Phase 1: Days 1–30 — Foundation

Running in parallel:
- [ ] Thumbnail color test — 5 videos per group

## Risk and Contingency Planning

### Model Accuracy Drops Below 90%
- Suspend greenlight recommendations — use model for directional insight only
- Diagnose drift: market shift, audience composition change, format change
- Retrain on most recent 50 videos
- Introduce new variables if the gap isn't explained by existing features
- Resolution timeline: 1–2 weeks

### Team Performance Issue
- Direct feedback in week one of identifying the issue — specific and documented
- 30-day improvement plan with measurable targets
- Decision at 60 days: keep, reassign, or part ways
- Address quickly — team dysfunction compounds

### Stakeholder Misalignment
- Identify the specific concern: "What evidence would shift your view?"
- Design a low-risk micro-test to address it directly
- Build internal champions at the team lead level
- Let data resolve disagreements, not repeated arguments

### International Launch Underperforms
- Diagnose root cause: localization quality, casting, timing, market-specific factors
- Assess whether to pivot to a different market first
- Adjust timeline — Year 2 delivery is still a strong outcome
- The $1.5B opportunity remains valid regardless of first-market execution
